Our activity is insured

The broker's professional liability is insured by JSC Victoria IC for the insurance amount of 833,250,000.00 tenge (the total annual limit for all requirements), but not more than 664,075,000.00 tenge for each and every insurance event.

Contract series No. 113 of voluntary professional liability insurance dated 08.28.2019, for the period from August 29, 2019 to August 28, 2020.

The object of insurance is the property interests of the Insured (Assured) related to its obligation established by the civil legislation of the Republic of Kazakhstan to compensate for harm caused to life, health and / or property of third parties as a result of professional activities carried out by the Insured (Assured).

An insured event is the fact of establishing the professional liability of the Insured (Assured) for compensation for harm caused to life, health and / or property of third parties and the obligation of the Insured (Assured) to compensate for the harm caused to the property interests of the victims as a result of activities carried out by the Insured (Assured) on the basis of a court decision that has entered into legal force, or a voluntary plea of guilt by the Insured, with the written consent of the Insurer.
